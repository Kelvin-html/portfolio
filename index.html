About

I’m Kelvin — a frontend developer with a serious thing for clean UI and websites that actually work.

Here’s what I do: I take clunky interfaces and turn them into responsive, intuitive, fast-loading experiences. The kind that feel smooth on mobile and don't scream “I was built in 2013.”

I’m not just stacking divs and calling it design — I build real products with React, Tailwind, and just enough JavaScript to keep things smart, not bloated.

I'm currently looking for teams that care about quality — not just deadlines. Junior roles, internships, remote collabs — if you're building something cool, let’s talk.

Outside of code? Gym. Binge watch my favorite series. And always spacing buttons 8px too perfectly.

I don’t just build websites. I build websites people want to use.
